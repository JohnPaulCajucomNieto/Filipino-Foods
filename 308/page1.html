Classic Filipino Foods

The origins of Filipino food lie with the Malayo-Polynesians, who were responsible for its most
common ingredient rice. Filipino food has been influenced by Chinese traders, Spanish conquistadors, and American influence. Spanish rule ushered several large changes to the cuisines of much of the archipelago, from the formation of the Manila galleon trade network to domestic agricultural reform. The galleon trade brought two significant culinary influences to the islands, Chinese and Mexican
